In Memoriam

FormedOklahoma City · 2009 The Loud Years2009 — 2013 Final ShowMarch 22, 2013 PersonnelJames Smith · Tommy McKenzie · Weston Lorance · Charles Whetstone Recorded byKevin Whetstone · Nice People Came · Wrote · ConqueredAnd then, the silence.

The dudes in The Boom Bang were buddies who liked hanging out and goofing around. So they picked up some instruments and started a garage rock / proto-punk / noise band. This is what's left.

Left behind Pizzapocalypse (2009) · Bummer Camp 7″ — 300 pressed (2010) · World War Fun (2011) · the split 7″ with The Copperheads (2011) · Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtlez (2012)
